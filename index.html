About us

In the era where technology is paving its way into the field of Education,there is a need to bridge the gap between the teachers and the students. LearnWise acts as that bridge!

LearnWise is a mobile application which provides a common platform for the teachers and all the students, the main focus being Std 9-12.

The teachers can upload course materials, create and schedule quizzes for the students, view each student's progress report, raise important discussion and much more with LearnWise.

The student community can make their exam preparation better by taking up the quizzes, getting evaluated, knowing their positions on the score board and thus performing well each day!

With loads of such features, LearnWise makes the learning process easier and efficient!
